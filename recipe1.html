Gnocci dish

Ingredients
  • 1 onion (or two shalots)
  • A handfull of cherry tomatoes
  • Any meat of your choice
  • Gnocci
Preparation
  • Chop the onion fine and slice the cherry tomatoes into quarters
  • Add a little bit of oil to a frying pan and heat up
  • Fry the onions and tomatoes
  • Add the meat of your choosing
  • When that's done add the gnocci and fry until crispy
  • Scoop everything on a plate
  • Enjoy
